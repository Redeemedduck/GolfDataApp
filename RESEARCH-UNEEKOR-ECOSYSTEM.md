# Research: Uneekor Ecosystem & Competitor Landscape

**Generated:** 2026-02-03
**Focus:** Native software, third-party apps, market gaps

---

## 1. Uneekor's Native Software

### VIEW Software (Included)
**Features:**
- Real-time ball and club data display
- Driving range practice mode
- Video replay of swings
- Basic shot history

**User Likes:**
- Clean, simple interface
- Plug-and-play setup
- Reliable data capture
- Video sync is helpful

**User Dislikes:**
- Limited analytics
- No historical tracking
- Basic range only
- No course play

### REFINE (Paid Upgrade)
**Features:**
- Short game practice areas
- 5 simulated courses
- Multiplayer mode
- Enhanced range features

**Why Upgrade:**
- Want more than just range
- Need short game practice
- Play with friends

### REFINE+ (Premium Upgrade)
**Features:**
- 15+ courses
- Advanced analytics
- Club fitting tools
- More game modes

### MyUneekor Portal (Cloud)
**Features:**
- Online session history
- Basic data export
- Report sharing
- Account management

**Limitations:**
- Interface is clunky
- Limited filtering
- No good visualizations
- Session dates unreliable (known issue)

## 2. Third-Party Uneekor Apps

### GSPro (Most Popular)
**Why Golfers Choose It:**
- Affordable subscription ($250/year vs $400+)
- 200+ courses available
- Active community creating content
- Works with multiple launch monitors
- Better graphics than E6 at lower price

**Analytics Features:**
- Basic shot data display
- Practice range mode
- No deep analytics

**Gap:** Pure gameplay, not analytics-focused

### E6 Connect
**Why Golfers Choose It:**
- High-quality course graphics
- Realistic physics
- Official course licenses
- Good multiplayer

**Analytics Features:**
- Minimal - gameplay focused

### TGC 2019 / TGC 2024
**Why Golfers Choose It:**
- Best course selection
- Course designer community
- Good value

**Analytics Features:**
- Basic shot data only

### Awesome Golf
**Why Golfers Choose It:**
- Newer, modern interface
- Growing course library
- Competitive pricing

## 4. Gaps in the Market

### What's Missing from Current Offerings

**1. Unified Analytics Platform for Uneekor**
- VIEW is basic, no third-party fills this gap
- GSPro/E6 focus on gameplay, not analytics
- No equivalent to Trackman Performance Studio

**2. Progress Tracking Over Time**
- Most apps show one session at a time
- No good trend visualization
- No goal tracking or milestones

**3. AI-Powered Insights**
- Arccos does this for on-course
- No simulator app offers it
- Opportunity for "AI Coach" feature

**4. Beginner-Friendly Mode**
- Most apps assume golf knowledge
- No skill-level presets
- Overwhelming for new golfers

**5. Data Export & Interoperability**
- Hard to get data OUT of systems
- No standard format
- Can't share with coaches easily

**6. Session Organization**
- Warmup mixed with practice
- No tagging system
- Hard to compare similar sessions

### What Uneekor Users Complain About

**From Forums (GolfWRX, Reddit):**
- "VIEW is fine but I want more analytics"
- "MyUneekor portal is slow and buggy"
- "Can't see my progress over months"
- "Wish I could compare to benchmarks"
- "Need better data export"
- "Want to tag practice vs warmup"
